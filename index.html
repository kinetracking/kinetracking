KineTracking

Velocity-Based Training, Kine Gate Timing, and Kine Posture Analysis

About

KineTracking is a performance tracking app designed for athletes, coaches, and fitness users. It supports movement velocity tracking, timing gate sessions, posture analysis, video analysis, and training review to help users understand and improve performance.

Features

  • Velocity-based movement tracking
  • Athlete performance monitoring
  • Kine Gate timing and device connection
  • Kine Posture video and live analysis
  • Real-time analysis
  • Video export and local result review
  • Simple and intuitive interface

Collecting and Using Your Information

Types of Data Collected

Usage Data

Usage Data may be collected automatically when using the Service. This may include information such as Device type, operating system, app performance information, diagnostic data, crash information, feature usage, and other technical information needed to maintain and improve the Application.

We use this information only for app functionality, reliability, troubleshooting, security, and improvement of the Service.

Camera, Photo Library, Video Import and Processing

KineTracking may request access to Your camera, photos, or videos to provide movement tracking, posture analysis, video import analysis, and performance review features.

  • Camera access is used for live movement tracking, Kine Motion analysis, Kine Gate setup, and Kine Posture analysis.
  • Videos are accessed only when You choose to import them.
  • Imported videos are processed locally on Your Device for movement, posture, and performance analysis.
  • We do not automatically upload imported videos to Our servers.
  • We do not share imported videos with third parties.
  • Exported videos, screenshots, and analysis results are stored on Your Device unless You choose to share them.

We do not use camera, photo, or video access for advertising, user profiling, or unrelated tracking.

Location Permission for Kine Gate

While using the Kine Gate feature, KineTracking may request location permission. This permission is used only to help nearby devices connect for timing gate sessions, including host and gate device discovery, Wi-Fi/local network setup, and timing communication between phones.

  • We do not use GPS/location permission to track Your real-world location.
  • We do not record, store, or upload Your GPS coordinates.
  • We do not use location data for advertising, marketing, analytics, or user profiling.
  • We do not access location in the background.
  • Location permission is used only while You are actively using the Kine Gate feature.
  • Local network details, such as device connection information or IP address, may be used temporarily to connect gate devices, but are not uploaded to Our servers.

On some Android versions, location permission may be required by the operating system for nearby Wi-Fi, Bluetooth, or device discovery, even when the app does not use Your physical location.

Local Network and Device Connection Information

Kine Gate may use local network or nearby device connection information to allow phones or supported devices to communicate during timing sessions. This information is used temporarily for connection setup and timing communication and is not uploaded to Our servers.
